Dream Interpretation ~ Frued's survey of dream analysis

The study and attempt to interpret dreams is a topic I did not expect to like. The first translated copy of Freud's work I could get hands on, The Interpretation of Dreams is a book filled with interesting arguments on dream analysis. Just like the author states, the analysis of dream-life is inadmissible to frame general rules without making provision for qualifications by introducing such terms as "frequently", "in most cases", and without being prepared to admit the validity of exceptions. In simpler terms, we cannot conclusively come to any theory of dreams, because there are always exceptions, but nonetheless, I was stuck to the book because of the interesting arguments being discussed. This post is for me to store such arguments somewhere I can always access.


Danush S, 17 March, 2021

  1. Freud, Sigmund. The Interpretation of Dreams. 1899. Franz Deuticke, Leipzig & Vienna.
